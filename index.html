The Alexanders of the world have had their empires, the Napoleons their continental ambitions, the Churchills their oratory and cigars. But what of the Akshits? For too long, we have remained fragmented—our shared nomenclature unspoken, our collective potential unrealized. This year, that changes. It is with great pride that we announce Akshit-Con '25—the first and only global convention exclusively for individuals named Akshit. Why Akshit-Con?

Much like the Non-Aligned Movement of the mid-20th century, Akshit-Con stands as a forum that transcends traditional divisions. We do not belong to one industry, one ideology, or one worldview. We belong only to Akshit-ness. Here, we can engage in unfettered discussions about the Akshit condition—its trials, its triumphs, and its implications for the future.

The Naming Tribunal

Proceedings follow the format of a traditional court, albeit with significantly more gravitas and slightly less legal validity.
